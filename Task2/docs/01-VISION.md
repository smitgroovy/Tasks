# Product Vision

## One-liner
A task management app that respects your time — fast capture, clear focus, real progress.

## Problem
Most todo apps either feel like a blank spreadsheet (no structure) or a project management tool (too heavy). People abandon them because:
- Adding a task takes too many taps/clicks
- No sense of progress or accomplishment
- Overwhelm from seeing everything at once
- No differentiation between urgent and "nice to do"

## Solution
**Flowdo** — a minimal but powerful task manager built around three core behaviors:

1. **Capture** — Add tasks in <2 seconds. Quick-add with natural language dates.
2. **Focus** — Today view shows only what matters. Smart sorting by priority + due date.
3. **Reflect** — Weekly dashboard shows what you completed, streaks, and patterns.

## Design Principles
- **Speed first** — Every interaction < 100ms perceived. No loading spinners for common actions.
- **Progressive disclosure** — Simple by default, powerful when you need it.
- **Content, not chrome** — The UI disappears. Your tasks are the interface.
- **Delightful micro-interactions** — Checkbox animations, smooth transitions, haptic-like feedback.
- **Dark mode native** — Not an afterthought. Designed for both modes equally.

## Target Users
- Individual contributors who manage 10-50 tasks at a time
- Students balancing coursework, projects, and personal goals
- Freelancers juggling multiple clients
- Small teams (2-10 people) who need lightweight collaboration

## Success Metrics
- Daily active usage (users return 5+ days/week)
- Task completion rate > 60%
- Average time to add a task < 2 seconds
- NPS > 50
