# Networking Tips

## During the Meetup

- **Listen actively** — Understand what others are working on
- **Ask thoughtful questions** — Show genuine interest in their work
- **Share relevant experience** — Connect your work to theirs
- **Take notes** — Remember key points for follow-up

- **Connect on LinkedIn** — Reference specific conversation points
- **Follow up on promises** — If you offered to share something, do it
- **Share resources** — If you mentioned an article or tool, send it
- **Schedule coffee chats** — Build deeper relationships
